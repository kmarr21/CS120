Upcoming Album

Dog Rules

Anticipated Release: Fall 2025

Our debut album will be a selection of our best covers, alongside a couple of originals. Dog Rules captures the essence of Black Labbath — heavy, soulful, and unmistakably canine-influenced.

From crushing renditions of Sabbath classics to our own original compositions about loyalty, pack mentality, and the occasional chewed-up guitar cable, this album represents everything we stand for as a band.

Pre-orders and more details coming soon. Follow us on social media for updates!
