About the Band

Black Labbath is a Seattle-based cover band reimagining the heavy, sludgy, and soul-shaking sound of 70s and 80s hard rock — with a loving nod to our canine counterparts. Specializing in high-energy performances that channel the spirit of Black Sabbath, Led Zeppelin, Deep Purple, and other riff-heavy legends, Black Labbath strives to bring growl, groove, and a little wag to every event we play.

Whether it's a festival, wedding, dive bar, or dog-friendly brewery, the band is here to turn up the fuzz and unleash the howl. Each of our four members owns a black lab (because of course we do), and yes, the dogs often join rehearsals (and are always keen for pats!)
